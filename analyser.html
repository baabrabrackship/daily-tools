How to improve your match rate
1. Run the Full Analyst Report
It shows every unmatched subject grouped by common words. You'll see which keyword gaps account for the most missed emails.
2. Download the unmatched CSV
Every unclassified subject exported to CSV — open in Excel, sort by word frequency, share the top patterns with us to add to the classifier.
3. Common BM gaps to check
ambil · hantar · nak buang · ada kucing · ada anjing · nak serah · tak tau · minta tolong · tanya pasal
4. Realistic expectation
75–85% is healthy for a public mailbox. Getting above 85% requires adding common subject patterns unique to your mailbox — the analyst report shows you exactly which ones.
